Our Story

Like many modern love stories, ours began in the most romantic of settings: a dating app in the middle of a global pandemic. In 2021, we went on our first date over bubble tea and quickly figured out that we shared two big obsessions, hiking and each other.

From there, the miles (and memories) started adding up. We’ve explored almost a third of America’s national parks together, chasing sunrise views, collecting blisters, and surviving many cups of mediocre campground coffee. Someday, we hope to visit every U.S. national park and collect enough memories to officially call our love a national treasure.

In the middle of 2025, we got engaged on top of Half Dome in Yosemite National Park. There is no better place in the world to confess our love and dedication to each other than atop one of the most majestic and breathtaking places on Earth. A year and a couple of months later, we invite you to join us in celebrating the next chapter of our adventure as we say “I do.”
